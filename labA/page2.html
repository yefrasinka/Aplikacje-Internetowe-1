Rick and Morty

Introduction

Rick and Morty is one of the most famous duos in animated series. Their story is filled with crazy adventures across the multiverse.

Main Characters:

  • Rick Sanchez – a genius scientist with unorthodox methods.
  • Morty Smith – Rick’s grandson, who accompanies him on wild journeys.
  • Summer Smith – Morty’s older sister, who occasionally joins Rick and Morty on their adventures.
  • Beth Smith – Rick’s daughter, a horse surgeon who struggles with her relationship with her father.
  • Jerry Smith – Morty’s insecure father, often ridiculed by Rick for his incompetence.
  • Rick's Ship – a custom flying vehicle built by Rick for interdimensional travel.

Fun Facts

Rick and Morty are based on Doc Brown and Marty McFly from Back to the Future, but with a darker twist.

The pilot was written in just six hours by Dan Harmon and Justin Roiland.

Justin Roiland himself voices both Rick and Morty.
